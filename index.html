How to pace your Zwift Climb

  1. Connect Strava: Link your account so we can find your past Zwift climbs (like Alpe du Zwift). We use your historical hairpin data to build a perfectly tailored pacing plan.
  2. Pick Your Goal: Choose your climb and select whether you want to beat your All-Time PR or a Recent Effort. Enter your ultimate goal finish time.
  3. Plan Your Effort: Use the slider to adjust your strategy. A "Negative Split" means you will start slightly slower and push harder at the top.
  4. Start Riding: Launch Zwift. Right as your avatar crosses the segment's start line, tap START.
  5. Hit Your Splits: Every time you cross a hairpin or sub-segment banner in-game, tap Split. The app will tell you exactly how many seconds ahead or behind your goal pace you are!
  6. 🚀 Cockpit Mode: Tap the rocket icon! On a PC/Mac, it pops out a mini floating window you can hover directly over the Zwift game. On your phone, it creates a massive, distraction-free dashboard.
