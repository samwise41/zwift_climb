How to use the Pacer

  • Connect Strava: We will pull your Personal Records directly from your activities to calculate realistic targets.
  • Set Target & Split: Enter your desired total finish time. Use the slider to start faster (positive split) or end faster (negative split).
  • Ride: Press START when you cross the segment banner.
  • Track Splits: Press "Split" every time you complete a sub-segment (like a hairpin bend).
  • Record Power: (Optional) Type your average wattage into the box for that split to map your performance on the Comparison Chart!
