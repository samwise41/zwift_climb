How to use the Pacer

  • Connect Strava: We will pull your PRs directly from your activities.
  • Custom Route (GPX): Upload any GPX file. We will slice it into checkpoints based on your chosen split interval.
  • Set Target: Enter your desired total finish time and adjust the positive/negative split slider.
  • Ride: Press START when you cross the segment banner.
  • Cockpit Mode: Tap the rocket icon to enter a minimal, distraction-free heads-up display.
